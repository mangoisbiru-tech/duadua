WITHOUT THE MESS.

In Malaysia, slippers are our unofficial uniform. From the mamak to the mall, we live in flip-flops. But standard socks weren't built for this life.

I was tired of seeing the struggle: Y-straps bunching up messy fabric, looking awkward, or going barefoot and dealing with the heat. It just didn't look right.

Dua-dua is the "Slipper Soulmate." A Slate Grey split-toe sock designed in KL for a clean, minimalist aesthetic that fits your Y-straps perfectly.

The Founding Mission: We’re an independent Malaysian label building a community first. Once we hit 500 Founding Members, we move into production. No overstock, no waste—just the perfect sock for the people who helped make it real.
